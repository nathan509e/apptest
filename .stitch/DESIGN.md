# ScoreFlow Studio - Design System

## Atmosphere & Vibe
Professional, minimal, dark-mode-first. Inspired by modern, premium music software (like MuseScore 4 or Ableton Live). The design uses deep grays and subtle borders to create depth without relying heavily on shadows.

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: 'Inter', sans-serif
- **Headings**: Clean, slightly bold (600), varying from 24px to 32px.
- **Body**: 14px to 16px, readable and clear.

## Color Palette
### Backgrounds
- **App Background**: `#252528` (Deep dark grey/black)
- **Sidebar Background**: `#1c1c1f` (Slightly darker for depth)
- **Top Bar / Header**: `#252528`
- **Card/Surface**: `#313135` (Elevated elements like the "New Score" card)
- **Card Hover**: `#3a3a3f`

### Text & Accents
- **Primary Text**: `#f3f3f4` (Off-white for high contrast)
- **Secondary Text**: `#9ca3af` (Muted gray for subtitles and inactive icons)
- **Accent/Brand**: `#4285f4` (Google Blue, or a subtle active state color, like the blue line on active tabs)
- **Border/Divider**: `#3f3f46` (Subtle separation lines)

## Layout Tokens
- **Sidebar Width**: 240px
- **Top Nav Height**: 48px
- **Content Padding**: 32px
- **Border Radius (Cards)**: 8px
- **Border Radius (Buttons)**: 6px

## Micro-interactions
- **Hover effects**: Background color brightens slightly, mouse cursor changes.
- **Transitions**: `0.2s ease` on buttons, cards, and links to make the UI feel alive and hardware-accelerated.
- **Glassmorphism**: Not explicitly used; instead, we rely on solid dark blocks with thin borders to maintain a professional, distraction-free environment.
